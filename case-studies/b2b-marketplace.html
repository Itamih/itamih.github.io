B2B Marketplace Platform

How we built Fend.ai, a comprehensive B2B marketplace that connects startups with enterprise partners, featuring payment processing, analytics, and automated matching systems.

300% Increase in partner connections
80% Faster pilot programs
50+ Active enterprise partners

The Challenge

Fend.ai needed a platform to connect innovative startups with enterprise partners for pilot programs and partnerships. The existing manual process was slow, limited in reach, and difficult to scale effectively.

Manual Partnership Discovery

Startups and enterprises struggled to find each other through traditional networking, conferences, and cold outreach, leading to missed opportunities and slow partnership development.

Complex Pilot Management

Managing pilot programs involved extensive back-and-forth communication, contract negotiations, and progress tracking across multiple stakeholders without a centralized system.

Payment Processing Challenges

Handling payments between startups and enterprise partners required custom contracts, manual invoicing, and complex financial reconciliation processes.

Our Solution

We developed a comprehensive B2B marketplace platform that streamlines the entire partnership lifecycle from discovery to payment. The platform includes intelligent matching, automated workflows, and integrated payment processing.

Intelligent Matching System

AI-powered matching algorithm that connects startups with relevant enterprise partners based on industry, technology stack, partnership goals, and mutual requirements.

Integrated Payment Processing

Stripe Connect integration enabling seamless payments between parties, automated fee collection, and comprehensive financial reporting for all transactions.

Pilot Program Management

Comprehensive project management tools for tracking pilot progress, milestone management, communication logs, and automated reporting for all stakeholders.

Analytics Dashboard

Real-time analytics providing insights into partnership performance, success rates, revenue tracking, and platform usage metrics for continuous optimization.

Technology Stack

We built a scalable, modern platform using cutting-edge technologies that could handle high user volumes while maintaining performance and security for enterprise-grade partnerships.

Full-stack Web App
Stripe Connect
Analytics Dashboard
Machine Learning
Real-time Messaging
Cloud Infrastructure
API Integrations

Implementation & Results

The platform was built over 4 months with iterative development and continuous user feedback. We launched with a beta group of 20 partners and gradually scaled to over 50 active enterprise partners and 200+ startups.

Partnership Growth

300% increase in successful partner connections compared to manual processes, with higher quality matches and faster engagement.

Pilot Acceleration

80% reduction in time to start pilot programs, from average 3 months to 3 weeks through automated workflows and streamlined processes.

Revenue Growth

Enabled $2M+ in partnership deals through the platform, with automated fee collection and transparent financial reporting.

User Satisfaction

95% user satisfaction rate with improved communication tools, project tracking, and overall partnership management experience.
